Summary

The delamination problem is tackled in this study. Perovskite layers can peel-off at the interface between tin-oxide and fullerene

Prevention Methods

  • Control of the roughness --> more wrinkles leads to better adhesion.
  • Control of the sputter power density --> the less the power density the better the adhesion
  • Control of the temperature during the fullerene-evaporation --> the cooler the better the adhesion
